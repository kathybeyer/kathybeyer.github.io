title: "If I Were a Bird"
---

If I were a bird,<br>
I'd sing a song,<br>
And fly about<br>
The whole day long,<br>
And when the night came,<br>
Go to rest<br>
Up in my cozy little nest<br>
-Mrs Ferris Poem
